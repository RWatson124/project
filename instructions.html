How to Play

Follow the instructions to play the game!

1. First, choose your character on the selection page. You will then be given a random selection of weapons, rooms and characters.

2. To begin playing, click the dice button to roll the dice and use the arrows to move the given number across the board.

3. When you land on a room, you can make a guess using the field shown. You will told if any of your guesses were correct.

4. Use the check list at the top left to keep track of your guesses.

5. Continue rolling the dice and moving across the board, using the rooms to make more guesses.

6. Make a fully correct guess within 10 minutes to win!
